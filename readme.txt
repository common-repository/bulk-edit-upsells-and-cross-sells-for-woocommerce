== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= 1. Is it necessary to have a paid ChatGPT account to access the AI features? =
Absolutely! To utilize the AI-powered product suggestions, you must have a paid ChatGPT account. This includes generating an API key and purchasing credits from OpenAI.

= 2. Can I refine AI product recommendations for more accurate suggestions? =
Yes, you can fine-tune product suggestions by including more product details in your AI prompt. In the free version of Bulk Edit Upsells and Cross-sells for WooCommerce, you can only select **Product Name and Product Description** as Product Details. The premium version also offers you the **Product URL and Product Price** to include in your AI prompt to get the most relevant WooCommerce product suggestions.

= 3. Can I customize the AI prompt for product suggestions? =
The [Related Products Pro for WooCommerce](https://www.saffiretech.com/woocommerce-related-products-pro/?utm_source=wp_plugin&utm_medium=listing&utm_campaign=free2pro&utm_id=c1&utm_term=related-product-pro-for-woocommerce&utm_content=beucw) allows you to customize the default prompt to create tailored product suggestions that fit your store’s unique needs.

= 4. How many AI-powered product suggestions can I request at a time? =
The Bulk Edit Upsells and Cross-Sells for WooCommerce lets you request up to 5 AI product recommendations and the premium version lets you set up to **10 AI product recommendations** for **Related products**, **Upsells**, and **Cross-sells**. You can set these WooCommerce product suggestions for upto **50 products** on your store.

= 5. How does the AJAX slider improve the shopping experience? =
The Pro version includes a customizable AJAX slider, which enables faster page loads and a smoother browsing experience by showcasing an unlimited number of products without slowing down your site. You can show Product Ratings on Related Products, Show Sale price on Related Products and Hide Out-of-Stock items from showing up in the Related Products slider for all products.

= 6. Can I keep a log of all my AI generated prompts? =
Yes, the Related Products Pro for WooCommerce keeps a log of all AI-generated product suggestions, including details like date, time, and token usage, so you can monitor and adjust AI product recommendations whenever needed.


== Installation ==
1. Upload the bulk-edit-upsells-and-cross-sells-for-woocommerce plugin folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress
3. Click on the \'Settings\' link or in the WordPress dashboard click on Products > Upsells and Cross-sells
